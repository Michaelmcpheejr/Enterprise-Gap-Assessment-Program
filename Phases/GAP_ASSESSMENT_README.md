# GRC Gap Assessment Program

A 3-phase framework for assessing organizational compliance against NIST SP 800-171 and related frameworks (CMMC, SOX ITGC, HIPAA, ISO 27001, PCI-DSS).

Designed for federal contractors, defense subcontractors, and enterprises handling controlled unclassified information (CUI).

---

## Quick Overview

**What:** Complete compliance gap assessment methodology - from identifying gaps to executive reporting

**Who:** Federal contractors, enterprises managing CUI, organizations targeting CMMC Level 2 certification

**Frameworks:** NIST SP 800-171 (primary), plus CMMC 2.0, SOX ITGC, HIPAA, ISO 27001, PCI-DSS

**Scope:** All 14 NIST 800-171 control families (~110 controls)

---

## The 3 Phases

**Phase 1: Assessment Framework & Methodology**
- NIST 800-171 assessment procedures
- Risk scoring methodology (Impact × Likelihood × Regulatory Exposure)
- Control evaluation standards
- Multi-framework mapping

**Phase 2: Gap Analysis Procedures & Evidence Gathering**
- Step-by-step gap assessment (6-step procedure)
- Evidence gathering standards (audit-ready documentation)
- 8 detailed examples (simple, moderate, complex gaps)
- Quality assurance validation

**Phase 3: Risk Register & Executive Reporting**
- Risk register design and tracking
- Milestone-based remediation progress (not % complete)
- Executive dashboards and reporting
- Board briefings and audit committee reports

## What This Solves

**Problem:** Most organizations don't know their actual compliance posture. Gaps are identified ad-hoc, prioritized randomly, tracked inconsistently, and communicated poorly to leadership.

**Solution:** Systematic assessment methodology with clear prioritization, accountability, and executive visibility.
